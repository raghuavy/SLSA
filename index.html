Who we are

More than a club — a little piece of home.

We bring Sri Lanka to Minnesota — its flavours, its festivals, and the people who make it feel like home.

The Sri Lankan Students' Association exists to bring together students from Sri Lanka and friends of the island, to share our culture and support one another through the long Minnesota winters. From kottu nights to cricket watch parties, Avurudu celebrations to study breaks, we make space for every Sri Lankan on campus — and everyone curious about the island.

Whether you grew up in Colombo, Jaffna, or have never set foot on the island, there's a seat at our table. Come hungry.
